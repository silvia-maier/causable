Sparring partnerships

Develop new perspectives on strategy, innovation and development

Would you like to craft your own path towards leading successful strategic initiatives, change and innovation? A sparring partnership might put you a decisive step ahead by helping you anticipate what your teams need from you so they can deliver.

Sparring on strategic perspectives and their own approach and challenges can help leaders develop their own take on leading initiatives towards impact. If you look to develop or fine-tune your approach to designing structures and processes, and to building and leading resilient and high-performing teams that drive innovation and change, we might be a match.

Is this for you?

At Causable, we back you with in-depth knowledge on good governance from applying behavioral economics insights in various sectors and organizations. On the individual level, Dr. Silvia Maier’s perspective is informed by up-to-date foundations how to build workplaces in which people thrive and perform.

We work with those who lead, or are aspiring to lead more in their roles, and want to do so with grounded confidence and integrity:

► executive board members

► entrepreneurs

► change and innovation leads

► leads in cross-functional settings

► research & development leads

► data science leads

► engineering leads

Causable serves as a trusted space and sounding board for those who want to know what we can create and be as humans, in our own lives and for others:

  • Do you want to know who you can become as a leader and enabler of collaboration?
  • Are you heading new initiative or feel a need to reflect on your work?
  • Do you see a growth or innovation challenge for your organization, team, or own life that you want to act upon?
  • Are you an expert in your field who wants to support strategic initiatives, and would like to be heard better by others?

Causable means: What can be brought to life?

Using a systems-based, transformative approach to executive and leadership coaching developed at the University of St. Gallen, Dr. Silvia Maier supports you in finding a path to the solution you seek to build. When she holds space for exploring complex questions and settings, her clients appreciate how she accomodates fast thinkers and learners, and connects the dots quickly.

At the same time, she knows when to hit the breaks and go deep on a topic. As a sparring partner, Silvia appreciates deep subject matter expertise and makes it shine. She keeps a spotlight on clear communication, and supports you in keeping track of your overarching goals.

Strategy, business models, leadership challenges, organization, tools and skills, and the development of your leadership approach during your journey – let’s find out together what you and your team need to gain traction and generate impact.
